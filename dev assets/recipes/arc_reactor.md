### Requirements
- Iron Plates
- Magnets
- Copper Wire
- Battery

### Craft
> Electromagnetic Chest Plate

This doesn't power anything yet—it simply keeps the **Shrapnel** status effect from progressing.

# Gameplay

### Initial Power Output

**20 RF/t** (or your own energy system)

Can power:
- Helmet
- Magnet
- Flashlight
- HUD

Cannot power:
- Flight
- Repulsors
- Lasers

This matches the movie where the Mark I reactor had very limited output.

---

# Upgrading

## Mark II Arc Reactor

Requires:
- Better Coils
- Better Housing
- More Palladium

Produces approximately **3×** the energy of the Mark I.

---

## New Element Arc Reactor

Late-game upgrade.

Requires:
- Vibranium (or equivalent)
- Nether Star
- End Crystal
- Dragon's Breath
- Quantum Processor

Produces essentially unlimited energy.

---
